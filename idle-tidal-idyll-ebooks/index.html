Idle Tidal Idyll eBooks

Many of the projects in the works (all of them, actually) at Idle Tidal Idyll are inspired by fairy tales. Many of these fairy tales were published many years ago and are now in the public domain in most countries. If Disney made a movie based on your favorite fairy tale? There is a good chance there are many publicly available eBooks for you to enjoy. But some fairy tales do not have easily accessible eBook versions. When it is possible, your friends here at Idle Tidal Idyll would like to make some of these fairy tales available to you for free. See below for books that you can download, enjoy, and read with your family and friends.

The Shepherd of the Giant Mountains

by Friedrich de la Motte Foqué

  • The Shepherd of the Giant Mountains: a classic German Romantic adventure said to have inspired Lewis Carroll's "The Jabberwocky."
  • Follow shepherd Gottschalk on his quest to slay a ferocious griffin, become a knight, and win the hand of fair Adiltrude.

This edition features the 1846 translation by Menella Bute Smedley, a relative of Lewis Carroll, and is from the celebrated author of Undine, the inspiration for The Little Mermaid.
